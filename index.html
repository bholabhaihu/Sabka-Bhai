Our Story

Cakes N Bakes Patisserie Bakery was founded with a simple mission: to create delicious, beautiful cakes that make every occasion special. What started as a small home bakery has grown into a beloved local institution.

Our team of talented bakers and decorators are passionate about their craft. We use only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, to ensure every bite is as delightful as the last.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, wedding, or just want a sweet treat, we're honored to be part of your special moments.
